About

The Story Behind the Lens

Andrew Miller is a Philadelphia-based filmmaker with a background that bridges technology, performance, and storytelling. After a long career in information technology and software engineering, he returned to school to formally study film and television, bringing with him a practical, systems-oriented approach to production and post-production. His academic work builds on earlier degrees in computer science and focuses on hands-on filmmaking, with particular interest in directing, editing, and collaborative production.

In parallel with his film studies, Andrew is an active member of the Philadelphia comedy and improv community and a co-producer of live comedy events and festivals. That experience has shaped his interest in performance-driven narratives, ensemble work, and projects that balance structure with spontaneity. He is focused on developing short-form narrative and comedic work for festivals, with the long-term goal of producing independent film and video projects that are grounded, character-focused, and technically precise.
