About

The Story Behind the Lens

Andrew Miller is a Philadelphia-based filmmaker with a background that bridges technology, performance, and storytelling. After a long career in information technology and software engineering, he returned to school to formally study film and television, bringing with him a practical, systems-oriented approach to production and post-production. His academic work focuses on directing, editing, and collaborative production, with an eye toward character-driven narrative and comedic work.

Alongside his film studies, Andrew serves as CEO of Coin Drop LLC, a Philadelphia live comedy production company, where he produces shows and festivals that keep him rooted in the world of live performance. That connection to performers and audiences informs his approach to directing — particularly his interest in ensemble work, spontaneity, and the dynamics that make a scene feel alive.

He is focused on developing short-form narrative and comedic work for festivals, with a long-term goal of producing independent film and video projects that are grounded, character-focused, and technically precise.
